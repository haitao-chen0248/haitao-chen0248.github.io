### 1. Gigapixel Microscopy for High-Throughput Zebrafish Profiling
**SPIE Photonics West 2026** | *San Francisco, CA* | *January 2026*

**Description:** In this talk, I presented our latest work on a multi-camera array microscope (MCAM) system designed for 3D behavioral analysis. We discussed the reconstruction algorithms and the challenges of handling gigapixel-scale data for biological discovery.
